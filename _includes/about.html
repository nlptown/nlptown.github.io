About

Text contains a wealth of information, but it mostly comes in unstructured form. Natural Language Processing offers a variety of methods to extract this information: it helps us determine the topics that are covered in a text, the people and places that are mentioned, the sentiment that is expressed, and so on. In this age of unprecedented data growth, more and more companies are turning to NLP to make sense of their textual data or to interact with their clients in new and exciting ways.

NLP Town combines a decade of research and development in Natural Language Processing with over ten years of experience in software engineering. After having learned the tricks of the trade at five universities in five different countries, we are now applying our expertise to numerous NLP projects in industry. If you would like to make the most of your textual data, you've come to the right place.
