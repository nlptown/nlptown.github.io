Information Extraction

Information Extraction is the task of identifying useful information in unstructured documents. Depending on your task, this information can vary from definitions in legislation or entities (such as people, organizations and locations) in newspaper articles, to career information in CVs or judgments in court proceedings. Whenever information is expressed in a consistent manner, we can develop methods to find it automatically.

Semantic Search

Today users expect more from a search engine than a system that simply returns a list of documents with the words they typed in. Modern search engines scan their text collection for synonyms, learn the topics of a text, or identify entities and the relations between them. In this way they can present their users with more relevant and personalized search results.
